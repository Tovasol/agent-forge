# Skill: Service-business go-to-market

The motion: lead magnet → funnel → discovery call → proposal → close. Offer both "book a call" and "buy now" where the offer is simple enough.

First 10 clients — do things that don't scale:
- Recruit clients one by one, manually. Go deliberately narrow first to reach critical mass, then expand to adjacent segments.
- Target ~10 customers with a burning problem over 1,000 with a passing annoyance.
- Deliver an early-customer experience no big competitor can match.

Track AARRR (awareness, acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, referral), CAC, LTV. Systematize (SOPs, templates, portal) only once the motion repeats and you have results to show — that's also the trigger to raise prices.
