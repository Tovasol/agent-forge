# Skill: Business-model selection

Score candidate models against: recurring vs one-time revenue, cash-flow predictability, gross margin, delivery scalability / SOP-ability, founder skill fit, capital required, time-to-first-revenue.

Archetypes:
- Productized service (fixed scope, fixed price, recurring) — usually best for a solo/engineer operator: predictable, systematizable, SaaS-like cash flow.
- Credit/request-pack — flexible, good for variable demand.
- Retainer — steady but can creep in scope.
- Consulting/hourly — avoid as the core model; punishes efficiency.
- Project-based — good for the FIRST clients; define scope ruthlessly.

Recommend the model that fits the persona, margins, and operator skills, with rationale. Favor recurring + systematizable.
